## Quick Icon Generation

1. **Create a base icon** (512x512px) with your logo/design
2. **Use an online tool** like:
   - [RealFaviconGenerator](https://realfavicongenerator.net/)
   - [PWA Asset Generator](https://github.com/onderceylan/pwa-asset-generator)
   - [Favicon.io](https://favicon.io/)

3. **Export all sizes** and place them in this folder

## Design Tips for PWA Icons

- Use simple, recognizable designs
- Ensure good visibility at small sizes
- Consider using a solid background color
- Test on both light and dark backgrounds
- Include some padding (safe area) around your icon
